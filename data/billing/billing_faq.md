# Billing FAQ

## How do I change my payment method?
Log into your account dashboard, navigate to Billing > Payment Methods, and update your card information. Changes take effect immediately for future invoices.

## Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.

## What happens if I exceed my plan limits?
API calls beyond your plan limit will be charged at $0.01 per 1,000 additional calls. You'll receive email notifications at 80%, 90%, and 100% of your limit.

## How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel anytime from your account dashboard under Billing > Subscription. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. No refunds for unused time unless within the refund period.

## Can I get a receipt for my payment?
Yes, receipts are automatically emailed to the account owner when payment is processed. You can also download invoices from your dashboard under Billing > Invoices.

## Do you offer discounts?
- Annual subscriptions: 20% discount
- Non-profit organizations: 25% discount (contact sales)
- Educational institutions: 30% discount (contact sales)
