# Invoice and Billing Policy

## Invoice Generation
Invoices are automatically generated on the first day of each billing cycle. For monthly plans, this is the anniversary of your subscription date. For annual plans, invoices are generated on the anniversary date.

## Payment Methods
- Credit card (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
- Debit cards
- Wire transfer (Enterprise customers only, minimum $5,000)
- ACH transfers (US customers only)

## Billing Cycle
- Monthly plans: Billed on the same day each month
- Annual plans: Billed once per year on subscription anniversary

## Late Payments
Accounts with failed payments will receive:
- Day 1-3: Email notification
- Day 4-7: Service may be temporarily suspended
- Day 8+: Account access revoked until payment received

## Refunds
- Monthly plans: Pro-rated refunds available within 7 days of billing
- Annual plans: Pro-rated refunds available within 30 days
- Refund processing takes 5-10 business days

## Invoice Disputes
Contact billing@company.com within 30 days of invoice date with:
- Invoice number
- Description of discrepancy
- Supporting documentation
